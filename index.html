Fast Workout Designer

THE APP

FWD (Fast Workout Designer) is an app that designs workouts to fit your needs. It’s hard to stay motivated to exercise, especially when exercising means doing the same old jog or set of bicep curls. This no-nonsense, easy-to-use app for your Android phone designs exciting new workouts in seconds. Stop making excuses, and start moving forward with FWD.

Set Your Inputs

Design your workout by entering the time duration of your workout, the equipment available to you, and whether you'd like to do a carido, High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT), or strength workout. For strength workouts, another menu will appear from which to select your targeted muscle group.

Get Your Custom Workout

Based on your inputs, FWD generates a workout for you from its rich database of exercises. If you don’t like the workout that FWD has created, then you can simply hit the ‘REGENERATE’ button and have a new one created without having to re-enter your inputs.

Follow the Exercise Tutorials

FWD provides you with step-by-step step instructions for every exercise so that you don’t have to worry when you see an exercise you’ve never heard of before. Learning new exercises to expand your personal fitness knowledge is part of the benefit of using FWD.




THE EXERCISES

FWD has a database of over 150 exercises to fit users’ needs and provide a variety of workouts. Physical Education student and long-time athlete Kayla Natarelli has used her fitness and health expertise to develop exercises for cardio, HIIT, and strength workouts. Strength workouts are designed to target the upper body, lower body, or core muscle groups and there are exercises that can be done at the gym or at home with without any equipment.

The exercises are stored in two databases: 'exercise details' and 'tutorial information'. The exercise details database holds the information about each exercise including if it requires equipment, the muscle group it targets, the reps and sets necessary for strength exercises, and the time duration of the exercise. The second database stores the tutorial information for the exercise. It contains easy-to-understand step-by-step instructions and accompanying photos to help you complete each exercise.

FWD uses document-oriented databases encoded in JavaScript Object Notation (JSON) [1]. Using JSON, the algorithm can easily retrieve exercise data to create your custom workout.

THE ALGORITHM

FWD uses a straightforward and effective algorithm to create a workout that fits your day. It does this by first reading the type of workout (strength, cardio, or HIIT) you selected and collecting all the exercises of that type from its ‘exercise details’ database. The algorithm then uses a process of elimination to narrow down the the list of exercises appropriate for your workout. If you selected a strength workout, the algorithm removes all exercises that don’t fit your ‘targeted muscle group’ from its list of eligible exercises. Once a list of eligible exercises that fit all your inputs has been established, the algorithm accesses the ‘exercise tutorial’ database to retrieve tutorial instructions.

The algorithm uses the desired time duration of your workout to determine the number of exercises needed, and randomly selects that number of exercises from its list of candidates to create your custom workout. Without your custom workout and accompanying tutorials created, the he algorithm passes the data to the UI to display your workout in a scrollable, clickable list of exercises.




THE INTERFACE

The User Interface (UI) was designed to abide by Google's principles of Material Design [2] with bold, contrasting colors, and intuitive user activities. Should you have any trouble creating your custom workout, FWD has pop-up notifications [3] to help you along the way.

FWD has layouts created using Android’s ‘ConstraintLayout’ class [4] that adjust to your screen size and rotate between portrait and landscape mode with your phone. Your custom workout and tutorial instructions are both clearly displayed in smooth-scrolling lists created using Android’s ‘ListView’ class [5].

With its sleek easy-to-use interface, we designed FWD to ensure it optimizes your workout experience.
